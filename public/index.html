We are a diversified business which specializes in Distribution, Hospitality, Agriculture, Power etc.

Prima Flour Wholesale & Retail Distributor - Galle

C. Kalansuriya & Sons holds the authorized distributorship for Prima Quality wheat flour in Galle, serving both individual consumers through our retail location on Dangedara Street, Galle City, and fulfilling the bulk requirements of bakeries and shops across the wider Galle area through our dedicated distribution network.

CBL Munchee Distributor - Galle/Habaraduwa

As an authorized distributor for Ceylon Biscuits Limited (CBL), we proudly supply the beloved Munchee brand products across the region, ensuring quality and reliable delivery of Sri Lanka’s favorite biscuits and confectionery to our retail partners and consumers.

Mega Stores - Prima Ceylon

As a key partner of Prima Ceylon, we manage one of their select mega stores nationwide, serving as the central distribution point for wheat flour to all distributors throughout the Southern region.

Tea plantation

For more than three decades and a half, our passion for the art of tea cultivation has been the cornerstone of our successful and thriving tea estate in the low country, building a rich heritage of quality tea production and sustainable practices rooted in care.

Hospitality:
Gloria Grand Hotel Unawatuna

For a decade, we have successfully operated one of the consistently top-rated hotels in the prime area of Unawatuna, demonstrating resilience and adaptability through significant industry challenges such as the COVID-19 pandemic.

Fuel Distribution - IOC

As a long-standing business partner with IOC in the petroleum retail industry, our company brings over a decade of operational experience, successfully navigating industry complexities and ensuring the sustained management of our fuel station which is located in a prime location in Galle.

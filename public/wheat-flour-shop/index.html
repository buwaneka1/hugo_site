Prima Wheat Flour Shop

Authorized Wholesale & Retail Distributor

Welcome to C. Kalansuriya & Sons’ dedicated Prima Wheat Flour Shop. As a key partner of Prima Ceylon, we manage one of their select mega stores nationwide, serving as the central distribution point for wheat flour to all distributors throughout the Southern region.

We serve both individual consumers through our retail location on Dangedara Street, Galle City, and fulfill the bulk requirements of bakeries and shops across the wider Galle area.
